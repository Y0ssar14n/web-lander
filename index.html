that closes the gap between cabled ground and networked sky. Upload is simple. If you ever find yourself in doubt, fall back on that phrase. It will generally bring you to the right place and help you to write the right thing. With that in mind, we can allow ourselves to treat the upload as something technical, something to write poems with. From the poetâ€™s point of view, the upload is a work that accomplishes basically two things: first, as with any computer network, the act of uploading moves existing information from one system to another. Second, the information it moves is real information, that is, it can be spoken. Taken together, these three related concepts (move, info, real) make the upload. Combined in the poem, they address conditions familiar to all readers and illuminate the direction of their creative energy: up, outward, laden with packets of possibility. The poems that you upload show us the cloud they connect to and do a little to close the gap between cabled ground and networked sky.
